Wave Tags is a solar-powered machine that invites people to leave messages in the form of wifi networks.

Inspired by the interventions people leave on walls around the city, it aims to open a new dimension of public space for this kind of anonymous expression. It is digital, but also physical and site-specific.

It is made to be hidden on street furniture near cafes, parks or anywhere that people usually search for wifi. Anyone with a smartphone can connect to it and leave their ideas, that will be propagated around the area as a new wifi hotspot.
